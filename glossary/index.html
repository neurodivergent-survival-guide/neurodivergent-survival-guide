Glossary

Words to describe your experiences

Many neurodivergent experiences don't have common words to describe them. This glossary helps you find language for what you're going through, understand terms you encounter elsewhere, and communicate more clearly about your needs.

Having words for your experiences can be incredibly validating - suddenly realizing there's a name for that thing you thought was just you. Each term includes a clear definition, how it might show up in daily life, and connections to related concepts.
