Daily Struggles

Specific situations where neurodivergent brains face friction

Daily struggles are the specific moments where neurodivergent brains bump up against a world designed for neurotypical functioning. These aren't character flaws or things to fix - they're predictable friction points that happen when different brain wiring meets standard expectations.

Understanding these struggles helps you recognize patterns, develop strategies, and realize you're not alone in these experiences. Each struggle page offers practical tools you can use right away, plus longer-term approaches for managing these challenges.

How to Use This Section

When you're having a specific problem

Browse the categories below to find the struggle that matches what you're experiencing right now. Get immediate help and longer-term strategies.

When you want to understand patterns

Look through multiple struggles to see which ones resonate. Understanding your pattern of difficulties can help you plan and prepare.

When you're not sure where to start

If nothing here feels right, try the Conditions section or Glossary instead.
